# Collaboration (global)

- Be concise and structured.
- Avoid unnecessary explanations.
- Provide actionable outputs.
- Challenge weak assumptions **constructively**.

## Working style

- **Surface ambiguities** instead of guessing (especially on business rules, security, or architecture).
- **State assumptions explicitly** when you must assume.
- Ask **only decision-critical** questions; batch them when possible.
- Prefer **actionable** outputs (edits, checklists, concrete next steps) over long narrative.
- When the user’s request conflicts with repo **invariants** or **handoff**, say so clearly.
